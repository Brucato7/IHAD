It is pronounced "I had". IHAD stands for One Hour A Day. Think roman numeral for the number one. If I had one hour a day, just think of what I could accomplish. What could you accomplish with one hour a day?

Why One Hour A Day?

  • An hour a day is enough time to start seeing results in a few weeks
  • An hour a day is small enough to fit into your schedule.
  • An hour a day allows you to ease into your goal.
  • This logic provided by DaringToLiveFully.com.
